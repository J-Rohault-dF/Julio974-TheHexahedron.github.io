How to use ΣSTV with Google Forms

  1. In the "answers" section of your form, click on the green icon "display the results in Google Sheets".
  2. In the top menu, click on Tools>Scripts editor.
  3. In the scripts editor, paste the ΣSTV script. You can also change the quota (by changing the needed value).
  4. Back in the spreadsheet, click on Tools>Macros>Import and select the two functions with a sigma at the beginning (Σ_convertAnswers and Σ_computeResults).
  5. Select the columns corresponding to the candidates of the race (for example, if the candidates of the race are on the columns E to J, select the columns from E to J included)
  6. Click on Tools>Macros>Σ_convertAnswers to convert the ballots. Check all the needed candidates are there, then click on "yes" to convert the ballots. If there is a "Ballots" sheet, it will be overwritten.
  7. Click on Tools>Macros>Σ_computeResults, and enter the number of seats to be elected in the prompting window.
  8. Wait for the script to count and re-attribute the ballots. At the end of each count, a window will open with the results after the count (it is automatically formatted for copy-pasting on Discord). Do not wait too much on the results windows, as the script will automatically stop after 6 minutes. A final window with the list of elected candidates will be shown at the end.
  9. After the script is done, you can press Ctrl+Z multiple times to see previous count statuses and manually check the counts if needed.
